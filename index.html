    About GGP

    Green Girls Platform (GGP) is a non-profit young women-led organization that works with girls and young women aged 8-35 to address the unique challenges they face due to climate change in Malawi. Our focus is on empowering these individuals through education, advocacy, and community engagement.

    Climate Education

    To ensure girls and young women have their capacity strengthened around environmental and climate change issues.

    SRHR and Mental Health
